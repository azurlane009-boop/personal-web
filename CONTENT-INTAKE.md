## Buoc tiep theo

Khi ban dien xong file nay, minh co the:

1. Chuyen no thanh data structure da ngon ngu trong code.
2. Them nut chuyen ngon ngu `VI / EN / JA / ZH`.
3. Noi website hien tai vao bo data moi ma khong phai viet lai tu dau.
